🛡️ Reliability of Cloud-Based Passes

Unlike a paper pass that can be lost, torn, or forgotten at home, your digital pass lives permanently in secure cloud storage. It's tied to your account, not a physical object.

  • Re-download your pass PDF anytime, unlimited times
  • Real-time updates the moment your pass is approved or renewed
  • Built-in validation prevents incorrect or duplicate submissions
  • Network error handling keeps the app usable even on flaky connections

📈 Scalability with Firebase

This platform runs on Firebase, Google's serverless application backend, which automatically scales to handle demand spikes without any manual server management.

  • Firestore queries are indexed and targeted for fast reads
  • Pagination keeps large datasets fast to browse
  • Firebase Hosting serves the app over a global CDN
  • Authentication scales to millions of users out of the box
